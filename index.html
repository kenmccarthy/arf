Key Concepts: The Two-Track Approach

The framework centres on a two-track approach to assessment design, allowing educators to choose the level of AI engagement appropriate for their learning outcomes.

Track 1: AI-Restricted

Assessments designed to minimise or eliminate AI use. These tasks focus on demonstrating individual competence through controlled conditions, such as invigilated exams, in-class activities, or live presentations. AI-restricted assessments are appropriate when authentic human skill demonstration is essential to the learning outcomes.

Track 2: AI-Integrated

Assessments that intentionally incorporate AI as a tool within the learning and assessment process. Students engage with AI transparently, demonstrating critical evaluation, ethical use, and the ability to build on AI outputs. AI-integrated assessments prepare students for professional environments where AI collaboration is expected.
